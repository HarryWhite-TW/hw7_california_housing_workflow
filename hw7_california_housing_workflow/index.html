CRISP-DM

One command, six stages

01 UnderstandDefine the regression target and learning scope.
02 InspectValidate schema, rows, distributions, and missing values.
03 PrepareImpute numerical and categorical values, then one-hot encode.
04 ModelFit LinearRegression inside a saved preprocessing pipeline.
05 EvaluateMeasure test R², MAE, RMSE, and K-fold stability.
06 PublishGenerate model, tables, report, images, and site data.
